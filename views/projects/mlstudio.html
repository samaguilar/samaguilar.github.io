MLStudio

Design & Development

The Challenge

As our final project for Introduction to Programming, a couple of friends and I decided we build something that wasn't meant to run on MATLAB. The idea? A music visualizer that would take any song, and play it back with a cool visual moving to the frequency of the song.

The Design

The way it works is that we take the audio file's sample, and plot the frequencies at any given time, 30 times a second, allowing MATLAB to produce a remarkably smooth graph. MLStudio also offers over 8 unique visualizations including ColorBoom™, which matches the current "mood" of the song to a particular color. If the song is on the more aggresive side, expect to see reds and yellows. Play a song, or even an entire album. The choice is yours. MLStudio makes it easy to enjoy your favorite songs in an interesting new way.
